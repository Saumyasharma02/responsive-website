About Us


Nestled in the serene hills of Devprayag, where the sacred rivers Alaknanda and Bhagirathi meet to form the Ganges, Cinnamon Tree offers a tranquil culinary escape infused with natural beauty and rich tradition. Our restaurant blends the essence of local flavors with global inspirations, serving thoughtfully curated vegetarian dishes made from fresh, seasonal ingredients. Whether you're a traveler seeking warmth or a local looking for comfort, Cinnamon Tree invites you to unwind, savor, and celebrate food in a space where nature and taste harmoniously coexist.
